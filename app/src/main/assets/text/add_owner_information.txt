<h5>How to add Owner information?</h5>
Adding your Owner info in android
phone is so easy. You just need to open
your android phone settings In 5.0 Lollipop :G to Settings-
Security-> Owner info. Now add your
Owner info.
You can see this info in your android
mobile lock screen.

Open your android mobiles settings and
select Lock screen.
In Lock screen scroll down and select
Lock screen Message.

Now write down your owner info.
