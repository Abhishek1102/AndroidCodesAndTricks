<h5> How To Record Your Android
Screen?</h5>

Now its very easy to Record Your
Android Phone Screen.
Just Follow me.
Open Your Play Store and Then Install
AZ Screen Recorder.
After Installation Complete Open Az
Screen Recorder.
Now Click on Red icon to start
Recording.
After Recording Drag Down your
Notification Bar and Click on Pause
BUTTON To pause the Recording or
Click on Stop Button to Stop Recording
Now Record Your Awesome talent and
Show it to your Friends.
Enjoy.
