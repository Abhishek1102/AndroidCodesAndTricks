 <h5>How To Use Wifi Hotspot?</h5>
 <ol>
<li> From the Home Screen, select the
Settings menu.</li>
<li> Click on MORE and then Tethering
and portable hotspot.</li>
<li> Then Check the box Wifi Tethering.</li>
<li>Then Click on Set up WI-FI hotspot.</li>
<li> Then Set User Name and Password.</li>
<li> Now Turn On Your Laptop WiFi.</li>
<li> You will See that your Hotspot
Wifi will Appear in your Laptop WIFI
Settings.</li>
<li> Now Connect and Enjoy.</li>
</ol>
