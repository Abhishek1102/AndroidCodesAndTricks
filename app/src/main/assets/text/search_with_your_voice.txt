<h5> Search with your voice</h5>
Have you ever noticed the microphone
next to the Google widget? Tapping the
microphone lets you search for anything
on your phone using Google powerful
voice analysis software.

 <h5>Click here to check out Siris beef with
Google Voice.</h5>
Some phones actually support voice
search without having to press any
buttons at all. If your phone is nearby,
you can test it by saying OK Google,
and then a request. Google Now has
a surprising level of functionality. Try
asking Google Now, Do I need a jacket
today?
You might be surprised by Google Now
answer.
